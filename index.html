About Us

How did we get started?

The idea of building an educational social media came while we were thinking of ideas for the project in a hackathon. We didn't work on this idea during the hackathon and continued with another idea. After some time, we realized that we could fill in the gap in the market for educational social media and pursued the idea. That's how our team started working on Edzu. We believe that this type of education is the future and an effective way to learn.

What are we working on?

We are working on the idea of learning by teaching. We believe that the most effective way to learn something is to teach it. Edzu aims to provide a platform to everyone who believes in the power of learning by teaching.

How will it help you to learn better?

When you teach any topic to someone, you have to prepare yourself for their questions. This helps you to learn topics with more dedication. Even if you cannot answer that question, at least now you know where you have to improve yourself. Teaching will also help you to gain confidence in your knowledge, and you'll be part of a community where you get to learn together.
